⬡ SAFETY PRECAUTIONS
🔒
Always check HTTPS
Never enter passwords or payment info on HTTP sites. The padlock must be visible.
🔍
Verify domain spelling
Attackers use "paypa1.com" or "arnazon.in". Check every letter before clicking.
🚫
Never click unknown links
If someone sends a link unexpectedly via SMS or WhatsApp — scan it here first.
⏰
Ignore urgency tactics
"Account suspended in 24 hours!" is a classic phishing pressure tactic. Pause and verify.
🔐
Enable 2-Factor Auth
Even if your password leaks, 2FA stops attackers from accessing your accounts.
📅
Check domain age
Phishing domains are usually less than 30 days old. TrapNet checks this automatically.
